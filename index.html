Introduction

Founded in ---- by Late Padmshri Kamalatai Hospet and Late Co-Founder Venutai Nene Matru Sewa Sangh (MSS) aspires to bestow better, affordable and low cost health care facilities for women and children of the marginalized community. Over the years, MSS has added a wide variety of other services to its ambit of work including. Maternity Home and , Institute of Social Work, School for mentally Retarded, Home for the Aged, Child Adoption Services, Working Women’s Hostel, Family Counseling Center.
